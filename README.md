# SaaS-Revenue-ARPPU-Analytics-Tableau
Revenue, Paid Users and ARPPU analysis with monthly trends by product and location

## 📊 Project Overview
Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing SaaS revenue performance, paid users 
and ARPPU metrics with monthly trends by product and location.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Tableau Public** — interactive dashboard and visualizations
- **Excel / CSV** — data preparation

## 📈 Key Metrics
- Total Revenue by software and month
- Revenue distribution by location (APAC, EMEA, USA)
- Monthly revenue share by location
- ARPPU trends over time

## 🔍 Key Findings
- USA dominates total revenue (~300K), far ahead of EMEA (~50K) and APAC (~10K)
- Highest monthly revenue recorded in March (34,125) and October (32,475)
- Revenue dipped significantly in June (18,960) — lowest point of the year
- USA shows highest revenue variance (box plot range 10–25), indicating inconsistent deal sizes
- Monthly revenue share remains relatively stable at 7–8% across most months
- Marketing Automation is the top performing product across all regions
